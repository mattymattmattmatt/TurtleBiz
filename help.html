Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our products on the Shop page, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

3. How can I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website.

4. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Please visit our Returns page for more information.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the Contact page, or email us directly at support@turtlebiz.com.

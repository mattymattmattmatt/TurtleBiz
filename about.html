About Turtle Biz

Our Story

Turtle Biz was founded in 2024 by a group of turtle enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for these amazing creatures with the world. Our mission is to provide high-quality, turtle-themed merchandise that brings joy to turtle lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

We are committed to promoting awareness and conservation efforts for turtles globally. A portion of every purchase goes towards organizations dedicated to protecting turtle habitats and supporting research initiatives.

Why Choose Us?

  • Unique and high-quality products
  • Passionate and dedicated team
  • Supporting turtle conservation efforts
  • Excellent customer service
